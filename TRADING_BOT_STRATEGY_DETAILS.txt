TRADING BOT STRATEGY DETAILS
============================

The project contains three strategy paths:

1. Professional multi-timeframe strategy, now the local demo default
2. Legacy SMA strategy, retained but not the default
3. Offline historical machine-learning research models


PROFESSIONAL MULTI-TIMEFRAME STRATEGY
=====================================

- 4H bias is preferred; 1H bias is the fallback when 4H history is insufficient.
- 5M is the setup and market-structure timeframe.
- 1M is used only for optional confirmation and precision.
- A liquidity sweep is mandatory.
- BOS or CHoCH after the sweep is mandatory.
- A retraced fair value gap or order block is mandatory.
- A bullish/bearish confirmation candle from the zone is mandatory.
- Spread/ATR and volatility regime checks are mandatory.
- Approved London/New York session is mandatory.
- High-impact-news calendar availability is mandatory and fails closed.
- Stop is beyond the sweep/order-block structure with an ATR minimum.
- Fifty percent is taken at 1R.
- The remaining stop moves to breakeven.
- Final target is valid liquidity or approximately 2R.
- Risk is 0.2% per trade.
- Daily realized-loss cap is 1%.
- Maximum one open position and three daily entries.
- Rejected ML artifacts cannot be loaded as confirmation.
- Live approval is false.


1. DEPLOYED IG DEMO BOT
=======================

ENTRY RULE
----------

BUY:
- The 10-period simple moving average (SMA 10) must be above SMA 25.
- Trend strength must be at least 0.30.

SELL:
- SMA 10 must be below SMA 25.
- Trend strength must be at least 0.30.

REQUIREMENTS BEFORE ENTRY
-------------------------

- At least 25 closed one-minute candles must be available.
- Current time must be between 08:00 and 18:00 UTC.
- Confidence must be at least 60 after learning penalties.
- Spread must not exceed 3 pips/points.
- ATR must be between 0.02% and 0.30% of the current price.
- The pair must not already have an open position.
- The proposed trade must not duplicate correlated currency exposure.
- The pair's 30-minute cooldown must have expired.
- Fewer than two positions may currently be open.
- Fewer than 10 trades may have been placed that UTC day.
- The risk engine must approve the trade.
- The emergency kill switch must not be active.

STRATEGY INDICATORS
-------------------

Used directly for live entries:
- SMA 10
- SMA 25
- ATR 14
- Trend strength derived from the SMA distance divided by ATR

Not currently required for live entries:
- Order blocks
- Fair value gaps
- Break of structure (BOS)
- Change of character (CHoCH)
- EMA 9/20
- RSI
- MACD
- Volume confirmation

NEWS FILTER
-----------

The project contains news, economic-calendar, sentiment, and crisis modules.
However, the deployed SMA entry function does not currently require an
economic-calendar or high-impact-news check before every entry.

It should therefore not be treated as having a complete live news filter.

TIMEFRAME
---------

- Live entry timeframe: one-minute candles
- Maximum rolling history: 200 one-minute candles per pair
- No one-hour confirmation
- No four-hour confirmation
- Snapshot prices are normally polled every 90 seconds.
- The trading strategy cycle normally runs every 60 seconds.

STOP LOSS
---------

- Stop distance: 3 x ATR
- It is not a fixed-pip stop.
- It is not placed specifically below or above an order block.
- A minimum ATR floor is used so the stop is not too close to the spread.

TAKE PROFIT AND EXIT
--------------------

- Take-profit distance: 6 x ATR
- Risk/reward ratio: 1:2
- It is not 1:5.
- No partial take-profit is currently used.
- No trailing stop is actively integrated into this live loop.
- IG manages the broker stop and limit orders.
- The bot can also close a position when the SMA signal changes direction.

RISK PER TRADE
--------------

- Central risk-engine target: approximately 1% of account equity per trade
- Maximum configured live trade-size cap: 1 lot
- Maximum simultaneous positions: 2
- Maximum daily entries: 10
- Pair cooldown after entry: 30 minutes

The bot does not intentionally target 25x leverage. Actual leverage and margin
depend on the position size, market price, account equity, and IG margin rules.
The broker-confirmed monetary risk should be checked before every order.

LIVE PAIRS
----------

- EURUSD
- GBPUSD
- USDJPY
- AUDUSD
- USDCAD

Not currently traded:
- Gold/XAUUSD
- Bitcoin/BTC
- Ethereum/ETH


2. HISTORICAL MACHINE-LEARNING RESEARCH MODEL
=============================================

ENTRY RULE
----------

BUY:
- The model's predicted probability of upward movement must be at least 58%.

SELL:
- The predicted probability of upward movement must be 42% or lower.

NO TRADE:
- Probability is between 42% and 58%.

- Entry is simulated at the next five-minute candle's opening price.
- Spread and slippage are added to the simulated entry.

MODEL FEATURES
--------------

- EMA 9, EMA 20, EMA 50, and EMA 200
- RSI 14
- MACD, MACD signal, and MACD histogram
- ATR 14
- Bollinger Bands
- One-bar, five-bar, and 20-bar returns
- Realized volatility and volatility regime
- Candle body and wick ratios
- Doji and bullish engulfing patterns
- Support and resistance distances
- Swing highs and swing lows
- Break of structure
- Change of character
- Fair value gaps
- Liquidity sweeps
- Bullish and bearish order-block approximations
- Volume Z-score when useful volume exists
- Spread-to-ATR ratio when bid/ask data exists

These are model inputs. They are not mandatory entry confirmations.
For example, a BUY does not currently require an order block, fair value gap,
BOS, and EMA crossover to occur together.

TIMEFRAME
---------

- Historical model timeframe: five-minute candles
- No one-hour confirmation
- No four-hour confirmation

STOP LOSS
---------

- Stop distance: 1.5 x ATR
- Not fixed pips
- Not specifically below or above an order block

TAKE PROFIT AND EXIT
--------------------

- Take-profit distance: 3 x ATR
- Risk/reward ratio: 1:2
- Maximum holding period: 60 five-minute bars, approximately five hours
- No partial take-profit
- No trailing stop
- A remaining position exits at the horizon close if neither barrier is hit.

RESEARCH RISK SETTINGS
----------------------

- Account risk per trade: 0.5%
- Maximum daily loss: 2%
- Maximum daily trades: 10
- Stop after three consecutive daily losses
- Maximum configured leverage: 20x

RESEARCH PAIRS
--------------

Models were trained for:
- EURUSD
- GBPUSD
- AUDUSD
- USDJPY

USDCAD was not trained because the supplied files with ZIP extensions were
HTML download-error pages rather than valid market-data archives.

Gold, BTC, and ETH were not trained.


IMPORTANT VALIDATION STATUS
===========================

All historical models are marked:

approved_for_live: false

Out-of-sample results:

- EURUSD: -60.35%, rejected
- GBPUSD: -16.09%, rejected
- AUDUSD: -58.26%, rejected
- USDJPY: +1.18%, but still too weak for live approval

The historical evidence does not currently support connecting these models to
automatic live trading.
